Microsoft Office

AVAILABLITY

  • Windows
  • macOS

Microsoft Office is a suite of productivity software that has become an essential tool for individuals and businesses alike. It encompasses a range of applications, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and more. With its user-friendly interface and powerful features, MS Office enables users to create, edit, and manage documents, spreadsheets, presentations, emails, and schedules with ease. Its versatile nature allows users to collaborate and share files seamlessly, whether they are working on a team project or communicating with clients.

  1. Go to the website: https://office.com
  2. Make sure you are signed in with your email id of University of Wolverhampton (account with the suffix: @wlv.ac.uk).
  3. Click on the 'Install apps' option.
  4. Click on 'Microsoft 365 apps', this will install a setup of Office 365.
  5. Run the setup to install all the Office 365 applications.
  6. Next, open up any of the office application (Word, PowerPoint).
  7. Sign in the application using your University Email Id.
  8. Restart the application after signing in with your Id.
  9. Verify the activation of Office by going through (for Windows: goto File > Account).
