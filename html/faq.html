IT Department - FAQs

To access the form, locate the QR Code available on the notice board. Take your device with a scanning app installed and scan the QR Code using the app's camera. This will redirect you to the form. Fill in the required information in the form accurately and submit it. Keep an eye on your personal account, as you'll soon receive a reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to proceed with the reset process. Remember, the QR Code is conveniently placed on the notice board for easy access to the form.

If an individual has misplaced their identity card, it is advisable to complete the requisite form provided. To access the form, locate the QR Code available on the notice board. Keep an eye on your personal account, as you'll soon receive a reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to proceed with the reset process. Remember, the QR Code is conveniently placed on the notice board for easy access to the form.
